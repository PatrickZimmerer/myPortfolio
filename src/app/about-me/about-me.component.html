About me.

" I'm a sports cars enthusiast and front end developer who loves pushing boundaries "

I STARTED

as a car mechanic in a car rental company, which combined my hobby with my job. Early on I realized that your getting payed very bad, so I quit and joined my family's business. After 5 years of hard work at construction sites and leading some of those, plus working long hours 6 days a week my brother came up with the idea of getting into tech.

MY SEARCH

after 1 year of research, looking into every coding course in germany and reading thousands of reviews, I felt like the Developer Akademie was the right place for me. They teach you the essentials you need to become a front end developer, aswell as working in a team in your own pace and building your own projects.

MY PASSION

was always computers, since I was spending over 90% of my spare time playing videogames. I was curious how software is being developed and always wanted to be a software developer, but was insecure because of the misbelief that you need to be a genius to be able to become a programmer. So I took the risk and quit.

MY JOURNEY

in the first month of learning I discovered new sides of myself, since I always struggled learning more than 30min a day in school. But when I started learning intresting things I was shocked that I was able to learn 8+ hours a day without getting bored or tired. That was the moment I realized I'm doing the right thing.
